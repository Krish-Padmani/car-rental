Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to rent a car?

You'll need a valid driver's license, credit card, and proof of insurance. International visitors may need an International Driving Permit.

Can I modify or cancel my reservation?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your reservation up to 24 hours before pickup without any fees through our website or by calling customer service.

What is included in the rental price?

Our rental prices include basic insurance, unlimited mileage within the rental area, and 24/7 roadside assistance.

Do you offer one-way rentals?

Yes, we offer one-way rentals between participating locations. Additional fees may apply depending on the pickup and drop-off locations.
